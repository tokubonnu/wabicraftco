About

Beautiful work deserves a beautiful frame.

WabiCraftCo was born from a simple belief: beautiful work deserves a beautiful frame.

These templates are made for makers — the potters, weavers, candle makers, and small-batch creators who pour care into every piece. Your Instagram feed should feel like an extension of that care.

Designed in Japan by a maker who lives surrounded by the slow, intentional beauty that inspires these templates.

Inspired by wabi-sabi and Japandi aesthetics — imperfect, warm, intentional — each template is designed to step back and let your craft take center stage.

No design experience needed. Just Canva (free version works) and your own beautiful work.
